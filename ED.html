Examination Division Notice

Internal backs will be conducted from 06 Oct -07 October 2017. All students are informed to ask respective HOD regarding schedule. NO FURTHER EXTENSION OF DATES WILL BE PROVIDED. All students are informed to report to college on 4th october. Form is available with HOD's.
As per the direction of UTU the examination portal is again open to fill the odd semester back paper examination 2017.all the students are informed to report to the college and fill their forms online.students can also apply for the papers which are in between of 22-07-17 and 26-07-17.
All students appearing in odd semester back paper examinations are required to fill examination fees at the earliest else their registrations will be cancelled.
All students of batches 2011,2012,2013 are informed to fill online odd semester back forms by reporting to institute by 1/07/17. please contact Mr Rakesh chandra, A. P, Civil department in this regard.
All students of batch 2012-2016 are informed to submit required documents for degree on urgent basics so that remaining degrees can be proocessed. Please contact Mr. Rakesh Chandra in this regard.
All students are hereby informed to follow the new fee structure as declared by university for Requisition of answer sheets / rechecking/degree/practical and theory back papers etc. Please visit university website link: http://uktech.ac.in/11042017-notification-regarding-revision-of-various-fee-of-university
Degree 2011-2015 : All Students of Batch 2011-2015 are informed to collect degree from Examination Division office Admin Block. Please bring a copy of challan and a photocopy of valid ID duly attested for acquiring the degree certificate.
All students who applied for requisition of answer sheets can contact Mr Rakesh Chandra, Assist Prof CE
All Students are informed that external practical examinations are scheduled form tomorrow i.e 08/12/16. Please consult respective HOD's for detailed schedule.
All students having Missing Marks in any Internal/External of Theory/Practical are informed to fill the current Application Format (CLICK HERE).So that necessary actions can be taken. Alternatively,Passout Students can Fill the application format and can Send the application forms through Speed post to: Mr. Ashish Joshi, Dy. Controller of Examinations,THDC-IHET. All applications should reach Examination division THDC-IHET on or before 5th Nov 2016 (By hand/post etc). After the due date no Applications will be entertained.
All students of 2011-2015 batches having percentage of 75% and above are informed to attend the concovation at UTU dehradun. Please register online at uktech.ac.in for attending the concovation.
For the smooth functioning of exam division THDC-IHET. All Students are informed to mention correctly date and time of examinations or back papers dates with proper subject codes and subject name who are applying for any kind of provisional/query regarding examinations(If Any).Without Proper informations applications will not be entertained.
